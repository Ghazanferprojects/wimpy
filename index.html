Frequently Asked Question

What is " The Wimpy Kidz NFT " ?
The Wimpy Kidz NFT is a spinoff of your favorite childhood characters brought into the Web3 space! These Kidz will grant you many different privileges ranging from discord roles, entry to giveaways, access to exclusive merch drops and, the best yet, access to the Wimpy Kidz Clubhouse! (Coming Soon)
When is the release date ?
As of now, there is no set date. The approximate release date is July 2022.
Which Blockchain will The Wimpy Kidz be released on ?
The Wimpy Kidz will be released on the Ethereum Blockchain.
How much will it cost to mint a Wimpy Kid ?
The mint price will be 0.15ETH
Is there a whitelist? If so, how do I get on it?
Yes ! We currently do have a whitelist and look forward to adding more Kidz to it. In order to increase your chances of being added to the whitelist, make sure to show your support for the project on our Twitter and Discord :).
What if I don't get on the whitelist? Will there be a sale open to the public?
Yes, we will initially have a pre-sale for all the whitelisted members and any remaining non-minted Kidz will be released in the public sale.
How many Kidz will I be able to mint?
There will be a limit of 3 per address.
When will my Wimpy Kidz be revealed?
Upon minting your Wimpy Kid, it will be initially hidden. The reveal will happen 24-48hrs after the public sale goes live.
